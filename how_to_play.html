Fish Hunter Game Rules :

  1. Use (Right - Left - Up - Down) to Move.

  2. If you Lose, Press Enter to Restart.

  3. Don't Touch Sharks or Whales.

  4. Each Fish You Eat = 1 Score.

  5. Each Pearl You Eat = 5 Scores.

  6. Each Diamond You Eat = 3 Scores.

  7. Each Golden Fish You Eat = 2 Scores.

  8. Every 50 Scores open a Higher Level
